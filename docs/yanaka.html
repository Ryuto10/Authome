Yanaka

Yanaka is a lesser-known but awesome place to explore. The atmosphere of old, traditional Japan remains strong. By strolling through historical streets of Yanaka, you could feel the nostalgia as it looked around the mid 20th century. Yanaka is especially beautiful at sunset when it transforms into a spectacular evening view. Highly recommended!

Yanaka Ginza Shotengai

Yanaka Ginza Shotengai is the 170 meters shopping street in Yanaka. Most of the shops are unique with no chain stores or fast food franchises. Yanaka is also famous as a town of cats. You could see many cats strolling around Yanaka Ginza. These all offer quaint and authentic experiences.

Yanaka Cemetery

Yanaka Cemetery is a major cemetery of Tokyo home to 7,000 graves that are surrounded by cherry trees. This place gets even more beautiful in spring, when the cherry blossom season comes.
