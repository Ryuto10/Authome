Harajuku

Harajuku is the place famous as birthplace of kawaii culture. There are many great spots to visit, including Meiji Shrine, Yoyogi Park, and Omotesando, a boulevard of luxury brands and other high-end shopping experiences.

Meiji Shrine

Meiji Shrine is one of the most important religious sites in Tokyo. The shine is surrounded by a 175-acre evergreen forest that is home to some 120,000 trees representing species found across Japan.

Yoyogi Park

Yoyogi Park is one of Tokyo's largest public park and a popular spot for people to escape the city. In the springtime it's a great place for hanami, flower viewing, offering an abundance of cherry blossom trees.
