Marunouchi

A swanky, most prestigious business area between Imperial Palace and Tokyo Station, Marunouchi combines soaring skyscrapers and historic landmarks with leafy parklands and contemporary lifestyle destinations.

Imperial Palace

The Imperial Palace is a large park area surrounded by moats and massive stone walls in the center of Tokyo. It is the residence of Japan's Imperial Family.

You can join a free 75-minute Official tour from the Kikyomon Gate for a comprehensive insight into the Imperial Palace. It happens every Tuesday through Saturday at 10:00 and 13:30. Also, you can join the other 2.5 hours free tour, Tokyo Free Walking Tour. It’s only on every Saturday at 13:00, with some irregular times on Sundays and weekdays, and may vary during holidays.

MOMAT (The National Museum of Modern Art)

Renowned for its impressive collection of modern Japanese art, MOMAT occupies a building designed by Yoshirō Taniguchi just to the north of the Imperial Palace. It showcases the history of art in Japan since the Meiji Period, with its collection displayed chronologically for visitors to easily follow.
